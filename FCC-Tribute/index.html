Behold: Chamoy on mangos.

This is a description of the vital history of Chamoy:

  • Chamoy is the juice from the fruit on the Tree of Knowledge in the Garden of Good and Evil
  • It was discovered by Portuguese missionaries in the 15th century
  • Franciscan monks thought it to be of divine origin and would use it as a salve on wounds
  • In the late 19th century, an entrepreneurial carnival performer saw the potential of the substance's sweetness to distract children
  • Today, all children in San Antonio, TX are baptized with chamoy on their 6th birthday
  • Y'all are probably already familiar with mangos
